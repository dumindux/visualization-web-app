Our Solution

Our solution consists of small handheld device capable of measuring hazad gasses and communicate with android device through usb cable. Android application show real time gas level and notify you if you are in hazard area. Using our web application you can visualize gas level details in real time in all around the word

Hand held device

This small light weight device consists of multiple sensors and it connects to android device using USB cable. You do not need to worry about charging.

Android app

You can see the hazard gas levels in ppm and in app graph to visualize variations of gas. It will notify you when gas levels increases to critical levels. App will automatically sync data with our onlince server for visualization.

Visualization

We use multiple ways of visualization to represent gathered data from large number of devices.
